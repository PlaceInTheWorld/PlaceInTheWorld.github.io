We got poisoned in El Nido

Traveling to El Nido, Philippines? Here's what happened during our two-week stay— stunning sunsets, beachside mornings, and a serious lesson about local water safety and food poisoning.

Top 10 Things To Do in Port Barton

Port Barton is a hidden gem in Philippines on the island of Palawan. Tourists, including us, are slowly uncovering its treasures, hence the place is becoming more and more popular. It makes for a perfect summer holiday destination for solo-backpackers as well as families. Here is a list of some of the best places to visit in Port Barton.

Port Barton, Palawan

Port Barton surprised me! From rustic beach shacks to kayaking adventures & Filipino karaoke parties, this hidden gem stole my heart.

Puerto Princesa, Palawan

I was excited for all the day trips, island hopping tours and adventures Palawan has to offer. Unfortunately, that didn't happen. At least not to the extent I would have wish for. Nevertheless, our stay in the Philippines, even though I still had an amazing time, was very different form what we imagined due to something we could have predicted but didn't. And it's not weather.

China

Shanghai

The biggest fail turned out to be such a blessing! Our first experience with a really bad hotel and really bad food. We made few bad choices on our first day in China but after relocating, we enjoyed our time in Shanghai.

Beijing

Despite a lot of security checks, we saw some of the most impressive building complexes in China.

The Great Wall

A perfect day trip from Beijing and easily my favorite site in China. I was impressed.

Terracotta Army

A quick and simple guide to the Terracotta Army museum and how to see the warriors.

Tianmen Mountain

The longest cable car ride in the world and amazing views from the Tianmen Mountaintop

Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon

A very spontaneous and chaotic trip at the beginning transformed into a very peaceful adventure.

Hike in Zhangjiajie National Park

Mountains in Zhangjiajie National Park were an inspiration for the environment in the Avatar franchise. They look otherworldly!

Fenghuang

Considered one of the most beautiful towns in China, it is especially worth seeing at night, when all of the riverside lights up and creates magical atmosphere.

Japan

Tokyo

The first stop in our gap year was Tokyo, Japan. We visited the main squares as well as the more traditional parts of the city. The highlight for me was The Making of Harry Potter studio tour.

Kyoto

More traditional Japanese, more tourists and more adventure. We saw Fushimi Inari Shrine, Otagi Nenbutsu Temple, and so much more.
