Fenghuang Ancient Town

Considered one of the most beautiful towns in China, it is especially worth seeing at night, when all of the riverside lights up and creates magical atmosphere. 

Start reading

According to a legend, two Phoenixes have flown into the city and didn't ever want to leave, as it was so beautiful. The city is named Phoenix, which translates into Fenghuang in chinese.

Statue of phoenix in Fenghuang

Phoenix City

The Fenghuang Ancient Town is considered one of the most beautiful towns in China. It is truly worth visiting, however for us maybe just for one or two nights. The Ancient Town is very traditional in its architecture and is visibly taken care of. The streets are very clean and new additions, like wooden water wheels, are being build all around. 

Restaurants in the streets of Fenghuang

A lot of tourist groups

However, the whole Ancient Town feels like it has been overtaken by tourism. Small-shop sellers approached us from everywhere trying to sell their products, and we've been photographed too many times by locals urging us to buy the photos. There are many shows performed on the river in the evenings from 6:30 p.m., including dancers and light shows. The riverside, which is the main and arguably the most beautiful part of the town, is filled with bars with live music and food markets. 

Riverside at night

Riverside at night

All we've been doing in Fenghuang is taking walks along the riverside. Especially in the evenings, when all of the buildings and bridges light up, the views are magical (maybe feel a little bit artificial, but still beautiful).
