I found myself in Finland.

We finished our south-east Asia travels with a flight from Vietnam (to Shanghai, then to Milan, then a bus to another airport in Milan, then to Finland). I have been struggling with my motivation and overall feeling pretty shaggy, my wisdom tooth has been growing and my my whole face and neck hurt from it. I should have probably gone to the dentist but it's getting better now, so hopefully it will go away on its own.

But I just want to go back to Vietnam. Or Philippines. Or Japan. I want to go back to where I watch the sunset on the beach, where the food is extremely cheap and it's so hot outside I decide to wear swimsuits instead of t-shirts. 

But I'm trying to enjoy my live here and be excited for what's to come - even though its quite hard, may I add.
